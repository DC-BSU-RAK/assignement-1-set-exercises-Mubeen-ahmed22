FAST & FURIOUS

Ride or Die

Plot Overview

The Fast & Furious franchise began in 2001 as a story about underground street racing in Los Angeles and has evolved into a globe-trotting action series about heists, espionage, and family.

Original Trilogy

The first three films focused on street racing culture, introducing Dominic Toretto (Vin Diesel) and Brian O'Conner (Paul Walker) as they navigate the world of illegal street racing and heists.

Later Films

From Fast Five onward, the series shifted to focus more on elaborate heists and global missions, while maintaining its core themes of family and loyalty. The crew takes on increasingly dangerous missions around the world while being pursued by various law enforcement agencies.

Current Saga

The most recent films have expanded the scope to include cyberterrorism, international espionage, and even connections to Dominic Toretto's past. The franchise continues to grow with new spin-offs and sequels planned.
