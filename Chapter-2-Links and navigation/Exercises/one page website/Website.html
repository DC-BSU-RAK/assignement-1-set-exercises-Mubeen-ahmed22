FAST & FURIOUS

Ride or Die

Overview

The Fast & Furious franchise follows a group of street racers, car enthusiasts, and outlaws who come together for high-speed heists and missions around the world. What begins as a story about underground street racing evolves into a globe-trotting saga about family, loyalty, and fast cars.

The first film was released in 2001 and has since grown into one of the most successful action franchises in cinema history.
